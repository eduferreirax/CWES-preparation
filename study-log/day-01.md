## Key Concept learned

### "Do No Harm"

One important principle introduced early in the course is the **"Do No Harm"** rule.

Just like in medicine, penetration testers must follow ethical guidelines and ensure that their actions do not unintentionally damage systems, data, or services.

Ethical hacking must always be conducted:

- Within the defined scope
- With proper authorization
- Without causing unnecessary disruption
